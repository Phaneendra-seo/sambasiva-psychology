FAQ

Common Questions

What happens in the first session?
The first session is about understanding your situation and goals. I'll listen to your concerns, ask relevant questions, and together we'll create a personalized plan for moving forward. It's a safe, confidential space.
Are sessions available online?
Yes! I offer both in-person sessions in Vizag and secure video sessions online. Many clients prefer the convenience of online sessions, and they are equally effective for most concerns.
How long does each session last?
Individual counselling sessions typically last 50-60 minutes. Couples or family sessions may run slightly longer, around 75-90 minutes. Training sessions vary based on the program.
Is everything I share confidential?
Absolutely. Everything shared in sessions is 100% confidential. I follow strict professional ethics and privacy standards. Your trust and safety are my top priorities.
How do I book a training program for my organization?
Simply reach out via the contact form or phone. I'll discuss your organization's needs, audience, and goals, then create a customized training program tailored specifically for your team.

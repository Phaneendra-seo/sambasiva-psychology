Therapy Methods

Evidence-Based Techniques

Proven therapeutic approaches to strengthen your marriage and relationship.

Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT)

Helps couples understand and reshape emotional responses to create secure, lasting bonds.

  • Identify negative interaction cycles
  • Foster emotional connection
  • Create secure attachment bonds

Gottman Method

Research-based approach to help couples manage conflict, deepen friendship, and share dreams.

  • Build love maps and friendship
  • Manage conflict constructively
  • Create shared meaning

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Identify and change negative thought patterns that impact your relationship dynamics.

  • Challenge relationship assumptions
  • Improve communication patterns
  • Develop problem-solving skills

Imago Relationship Therapy

Helps couples understand childhood experiences and how they impact adult relationships.

  • Understand childhood wounds
  • Heal relationship patterns
  • Create conscious partnership

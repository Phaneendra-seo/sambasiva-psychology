Frequently Asked Questions

Who should attend family therapy sessions?

Attendance depends on the issues being addressed. Some sessions involve the entire family, while others focus on specific relationships like parent-child or couples within the family context. We tailor the approach to your family's needs.

How long does family therapy take?

Most families see significant improvement in 8-12 sessions, but duration varies based on complexity of issues, family size, and commitment to the process. Some families benefit from ongoing maintenance sessions.

Is family therapy confidential?

Yes, family therapy is completely confidential. However, there's an understanding that what's discussed in family sessions stays within the family. We establish clear confidentiality guidelines that respect both individual privacy and family trust.

At what age can children participate?

Children as young as 3-4 can participate in age-appropriate ways. For younger children, we use play therapy and creative approaches. Teenagers can engage more directly in discussions and skill-building activities.

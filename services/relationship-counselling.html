Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do both partners need to attend sessions?

While couples sessions are most effective when both partners attend, individual sessions are also valuable. Sometimes one partner starts with individual counseling to work through personal issues before joint sessions.

How long does relationship counselling take?

Most couples see significant improvement in 6-12 sessions. However, the duration depends on your specific goals, the complexity of issues, and your commitment to the process. Some couples benefit from longer-term support.

Is everything we discuss kept confidential?

Absolutely. All sessions are 100% confidential. Information shared in counseling cannot be disclosed without written consent, except in specific circumstances involving harm to self or others as required by law.

Do you offer online relationship counselling?

Yes! We offer secure video counseling sessions that are just as effective as in-person meetings. Many couples prefer the convenience and comfort of online sessions from their own home.

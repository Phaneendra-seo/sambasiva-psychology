Frequently Asked Questions

How long does self-esteem therapy take?

Most clients see significant improvement in 8-12 sessions. However, building lasting self-esteem is an ongoing process. Some benefit from periodic maintenance sessions to reinforce progress.

Can therapy really improve self-esteem?

Absolutely. Research consistently shows that therapy, particularly cognitive-behavioral approaches, can significantly improve self-esteem. The key is consistent practice and applying learned techniques in daily life.

Is low self-esteem a mental health condition?

While not a formal diagnosis, low self-esteem often co-occurs with conditions like depression and anxiety. It significantly impacts quality of life and is absolutely worth addressing professionally.

Is self-esteem therapy confidential?

Yes, completely. All therapy sessions are 100% confidential. Your privacy is protected by professional ethics and legal standards. You can explore sensitive self-esteem issues in a safe, supportive environment.

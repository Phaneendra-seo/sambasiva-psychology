Anxiety Types

Anxiety Disorders We Treat

Specialized treatment approaches for different types of anxiety and stress-related conditions.

Social Anxiety

Fear of social situations and judgment by others.

  • Public speaking fear
  • Social avoidance
  • Performance anxiety

Panic Disorder

Sudden episodes of intense fear and physical symptoms.

  • Panic attacks
  • Fear of losing control
  • Avoidance behaviors

Generalized Anxiety

Persistent worry about various aspects of life.

  • Excessive worrying
  • Physical tension
  • Sleep difficulties
Treatment Methods

Evidence-Based Therapies

Proven therapeutic approaches to help you overcome anxiety and manage stress effectively.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Gold standard treatment for anxiety that helps identify and change negative thought patterns.

  • Challenge anxious thoughts
  • Exposure therapy
  • Behavioral activation

Mindfulness-Based Therapy

Develop present-moment awareness to reduce anxiety and stress through mindfulness practices.

  • Meditation techniques
  • Breathing exercises
  • Body scan relaxation

Acceptance & Commitment Therapy (ACT)

Learn to accept difficult emotions and commit to actions that align with your values.

  • Psychological flexibility
  • Values clarification
  • Committed action

Relaxation Training

Learn specific techniques to calm your body and mind during stressful situations.

  • Progressive muscle relaxation
  • Deep breathing techniques
  • Guided imagery

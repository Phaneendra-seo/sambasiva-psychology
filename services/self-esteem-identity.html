Therapy Methods

Evidence-Based Techniques

Proven therapeutic approaches to build self-esteem and strengthen your identity.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Identify and challenge negative self-talk and limiting beliefs that damage self-esteem.

  • Challenge negative thoughts
  • Build positive self-talk
  • Develop self-compassion

Self-Compassion Therapy

Learn to treat yourself with kindness and understanding, especially during difficult times.

  • Self-kindness practices
  • Common humanity awareness
  • Mindful self-acceptance

Schema Therapy

Address deep-seated patterns from childhood that impact your self-worth and relationships.

  • Identify maladaptive schemas
  • Heal childhood wounds
  • Develop healthy adult modes

Acceptance & Commitment Therapy (ACT)

Accept your thoughts and feelings while committing to actions that align with your values.

  • Values clarification
  • Psychological flexibility
  • Authentic living

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does anxiety treatment take?

Most clients see significant improvement within 8-12 sessions. However, treatment duration varies based on severity, specific conditions, and individual response to therapy. Some benefit from ongoing maintenance sessions.

Do I need medication for anxiety?

Not necessarily. While medication can be helpful for some, many people successfully manage anxiety through therapy alone. We can discuss medication options and coordinate with your psychiatrist if needed.

Is online therapy as effective as in-person?

Research shows online therapy can be just as effective as in-person sessions for anxiety treatment. Many clients prefer the convenience and comfort of online sessions from their own environment.

Is anxiety treatment confidential?

Absolutely. All therapy sessions are 100% confidential. Your privacy is protected by professional ethics and legal standards. Nothing discussed in therapy will be shared without your explicit consent.

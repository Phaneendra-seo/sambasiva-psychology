About Dr. Sambasiva

The Story Behind My Mission

Dedicated to helping individuals unlock their full potential through compassionate, evidence-based psychological support.

6.5+
Years Experience

Hi, I'm Sambasiva

I'm a professional psychologist and certified life skills trainer based in Vizag. My journey into psychology began with a simple yet profound realization: everyone deserves to be heard, understood, and supported.

Over the years, I've had the privilege of working with over 2,000+ counselling clients and training more than 22,000+ students across various institutions.

My approach combines evidence-based psychological techniques with practical life skills training, ensuring my clients don't just feel better—they develop lasting tools for lifelong wellbeing.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you customize training for our organization?

We conduct a thorough needs assessment, including interviews with key stakeholders and analysis of your organizational challenges. Based on this, we design customized content that addresses your specific needs and industry context.

What is the ideal group size for training?

Optimal group sizes are 15-25 participants for interactive workshops. For larger groups, we recommend multiple sessions or train-the-trainer approaches to maintain effectiveness.

Do you provide on-site training?

Yes, we provide both on-site training at your location and off-site at our facilities. On-site training is often preferred as it minimizes disruption and allows for real-world application.

How do you measure training effectiveness?

We use a comprehensive approach including pre/post assessments, participant feedback, behavioral observation, and business metrics. We provide detailed reports showing ROI and impact on organizational performance.

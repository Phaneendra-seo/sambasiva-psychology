Frequently Asked Questions

Who can benefit from life skills training?

Anyone looking to improve their personal and professional life can benefit. This includes students, working professionals, parents, and anyone seeking personal growth and self-improvement.

How long is the training program?

The standard program runs for 8 weeks with weekly sessions. We also offer intensive weekend programs and customized duration based on individual needs and schedules.

What is the batch size?

We maintain small batch sizes of 10-15 participants to ensure personalized attention and effective learning. This allows for better interaction and individual feedback.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes, all participants receive a completion certificate. Premium program participants receive an advanced certificate that can enhance your resume and career prospects.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you work with?

We work with students from Class 1 (6 years) through college students. Each program is specifically designed for age-appropriate learning and development.

How long are the programs?

Programs range from 6 to 12 weeks, with weekly sessions. Duration depends on the specific program and individual student needs.

How do you track student progress?

We use regular assessments, progress reports, parent feedback sessions, and measurable goals to track and demonstrate student improvement.

Do you involve parents in the process?

Yes, we believe in a collaborative approach. Parents receive regular progress updates, attend consultation sessions, and get guidance to support their child's development.

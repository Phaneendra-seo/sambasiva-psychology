Core Competencies

The 10 Essential Life Skills

Master these WHO-recommended life skills for personal and professional excellence.

Self-Awareness

Understand your emotions, strengths, weaknesses, and values to make better life decisions.

  • Emotional intelligence
  • Personal values clarification
  • Strengths and weaknesses analysis

Empathy

Develop the ability to understand and share the feelings of others for better relationships.

  • Perspective-taking skills
  • Active listening
  • Emotional support skills

Effective Communication

Master verbal and non-verbal communication to express yourself clearly and understand others.

  • Assertive expression
  • Non-verbal cues reading
  • Conflict resolution

Interpersonal Relationships

Build and maintain healthy relationships with family, friends, and colleagues.

  • Trust building
  • Boundary setting
  • Relationship maintenance

Coping with Stress

Develop effective strategies to manage stress and maintain emotional well-being.

  • Stress identification
  • Relaxation techniques
  • Time management

Managing Emotions

Learn to recognize, understand, and regulate your emotions effectively.

  • Emotional recognition
  • Emotional regulation
  • Healthy expression

Self-Esteem

Build confidence and develop a positive self-image for personal success.

  • Positive self-talk
  • Strengths recognition
  • Goal achievement

Decision Making

Develop critical thinking skills to make informed and effective decisions.

  • Problem analysis
  • Option evaluation
  • Consequence consideration

Creative Thinking

Develop innovative thinking and problem-solving skills for personal and professional growth.

  • Brainstorming techniques
  • Lateral thinking
  • Innovation mindset

Critical Thinking

Develop analytical skills to evaluate information and make reasoned judgments.

  • Information analysis
  • Logical reasoning
  • Evidence evaluation

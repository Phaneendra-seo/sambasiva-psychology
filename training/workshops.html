Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend the workshops?

Our workshops are open to everyone - students, professionals, parents, and anyone interested in personal development. Some workshops may have age recommendations.

What are the workshop timings?

Most workshops run on weekends (Saturday-Sunday) from 9 AM to 5 PM with a lunch break. Some 1-day workshops may be scheduled on weekdays.

Where are the workshops conducted?

Workshops are conducted at our training center in Visakhapatnam. For corporate groups, we can arrange on-site training at your location.

What materials are provided?

You'll receive comprehensive workshop materials including workbooks, reference guides, and access to online resources. A completion certificate is also provided.

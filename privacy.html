Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our psychological services. This includes:

  • Name and Contact Details: When you book appointments or contact us
  • Email Address: For communication and appointment confirmations
  • Phone Number: For appointment scheduling
  • Session Notes: With your consent for therapeutic purposes
  • Payment Information: For billing and processing
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our website and services

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide better psychological services:

  • Service Delivery: To schedule and conduct therapy sessions
  • Communication: To send appointment reminders and updates
  • Billing: To process payments for services rendered
  • Improvement: To enhance our website and service offerings
  • Research: To understand client needs and improve care

Information Sharing

We may share your information in these circumstances:

  • Medical Professionals: With your consent for coordinated care
  • Emergency Services: In crisis situations requiring immediate intervention
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or court order
  • Research Partners: For academic research with anonymized data

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmissions are encrypted
  • Secure Storage: Information stored on secure servers
  • Access Control: Limited access to sensitive information
  • Regular Updates: Security systems maintained and updated

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out: Decline further data collection and use
  • Complaint: File complaints with data protection authorities

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand website usage and improve services
  • Marketing Cookies: With your consent for promotional content
  • Cookie Control: Manage preferences through browser settings

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be:

  • Posted: On our website with effective date
  • Notified: Through email or website announcements
  • Continuous: Your continued use indicates acceptance

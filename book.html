Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical consultation last?

Initial consultations typically last 60-90 minutes. This gives us ample time to understand your concerns, discuss your goals, and outline a potential approach. Follow-up sessions are usually 45-60 minutes, depending on your specific needs and progress.

Is everything I share confidential?

Absolutely. All sessions are 100% confidential as per professional ethics and legal requirements. Nothing you share will be disclosed to anyone without your explicit written consent, except in specific circumstances involving harm to self or others.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment options including cash, bank transfer, UPI, and major credit/debit cards. Payment is typically due at the time of service. We also offer flexible payment plans for extended therapy programs.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel with at least 24 hours notice at no additional cost. For cancellations within 24 hours, a nominal fee may apply. We understand that life happens, so we try to accommodate schedule changes whenever possible.
